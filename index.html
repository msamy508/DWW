Everything you need to know about joining us

Not at all! We welcome paddlers of all levels, from complete beginners to experienced athletes. Our coaches provide training and support to help you learn proper technique and build confidence on the water.

We recommend consulting with your doctor first. Many people paddle during treatment with their doctor's approval. We can adjust activities to match your energy levels and capabilities.

Bring comfortable athletic clothing, water shoes or sandals, sunscreen, a hat, water bottle, and a towel. We provide all paddling equipment including life jackets and paddles.

The repetitive paddling motion helps lymph fluid move and drain properly, reducing swelling. Research shows that dragon boating is safe and beneficial for managing lymphedema when done correctly under guidance.
